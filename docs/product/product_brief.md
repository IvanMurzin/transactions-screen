## Elevator pitch
One paragraph. Imagine telling a stranger at a coffee shop what the
app is and why they'd care.

## Vision (12-month)
Where you want the product to be a year from now. Not a feature list —
a state of the world.

## Why now
Why does this product make sense in 2026 and not in 2020 or 2030?
Reference shifts in technology, regulation, behaviour, or distribution.

## Anti-vision
What this product will explicitly never become.
